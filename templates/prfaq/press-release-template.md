# Press Release Template

> Fill in the bracketed sections. Remove all guidance text before sharing.

---

## [PRODUCT NAME]: [ONE-LINE VALUE PROPOSITION]

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

[CITY] — [COMPANY NAME] today announced [WHAT WAS LAUNCHED]. [PRODUCT NAME] [SOLVES WHAT PROBLEM] for [TARGET CUSTOMER] by [HOW IT WORKS AT A HIGH LEVEL].

"[QUOTE FROM FOUNDER/CEO EXPLAINING WHY THIS MATTERS AND WHAT GAP IT FILLS]," said [NAME], [TITLE] of [COMPANY].

[2-3 PARAGRAPHS EXPANDING ON]:
- The problem in the market (with specifics — numbers, incidents, industry trends)
- What the product does differently (3 concrete capabilities, not marketing fluff)
- How it works technically (enough detail that an engineer nods, not so much that a PM glazes)

### Key Capabilities

1. **[CAPABILITY 1 NAME]**. [2-3 sentences describing what it does, why it matters, and what happens without it.]

2. **[CAPABILITY 2 NAME]**. [2-3 sentences.]

3. **[CAPABILITY 3 NAME]**. [2-3 sentences.]

### Availability and Pricing

[PRODUCT NAME] is available [TODAY / DATE] at [URL]. [PRICING MODEL — be specific: tiers, per-unit costs, free trial details]. [WHAT'S INCLUDED IN EACH TIER].

---

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Can a new team member read this and explain what the product does?
- [ ] Are there specific numbers, not just adjectives?
- [ ] Does the quote sound like a human said it, not a press release generator?
- [ ] Would you send this to a journalist without embarrassment?
- [ ] Does it describe a product someone would actually pay for?
- [ ] Are the capabilities concrete enough to write tests against?
