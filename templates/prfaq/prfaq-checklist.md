# PR/FAQ Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing your PR/FAQ artifact.

## Press Release

- [ ] **Title**: Contains product name and value proposition in one line
- [ ] **Opening paragraph**: Answers who, what, why in three sentences
- [ ] **Problem statement**: Uses specific numbers, not adjectives
- [ ] **Capabilities**: 3 concrete capabilities, each testable
- [ ] **Quote**: Sounds human, not generated
- [ ] **Pricing**: Specific tiers and numbers, not "contact us"
- [ ] **Availability**: Specific date and URL

## FAQ

- [ ] **Customer FAQ**: 6+ questions from the customer's perspective
- [ ] **Internal FAQ**: 3+ hard questions from engineering/business
- [ ] **No softball questions**: Every question is one a real person would ask
- [ ] **Specific answers**: Numbers, technical details, honest limitations
- [ ] **Exit strategy**: Data portability explicitly addressed

## Overall

- [ ] **The Two-Paragraph Test**: Can someone read the first two paragraphs and explain the product to a colleague?
- [ ] **The Engineer Test**: Would an engineer read the capabilities and know what to build?
- [ ] **The Investor Test**: Would an investor read the pricing and understand the business model?
- [ ] **The Journalist Test**: Would you send this to a reporter without revision?
