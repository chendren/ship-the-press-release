# Expected: An FAQ that includes uncomfortable questions

## Customer FAQ Must Include

1. "Why not [competitor]?" — with an honest answer
2. "What about my data?" — with encryption and residency specifics
3. "Can I leave?" — with export details
4. A pricing question with concrete numbers

## Internal FAQ Must Include

1. "What's the hardest technical problem?" — with an honest assessment
2. "What breaks at scale?" — with architecture limits
3. At least one question that made you uncomfortable to answer

If your FAQ doesn't make you slightly uncomfortable, it's not honest enough.
