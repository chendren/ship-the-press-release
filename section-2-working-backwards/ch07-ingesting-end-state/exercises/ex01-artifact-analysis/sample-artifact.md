# QuickNote: Voice Notes That Organize Themselves

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

QuickNote today launched a voice-to-text note-taking app that automatically categorizes and tags your notes using AI. Speak a note, and QuickNote transcribes it, categorizes it (work, personal, idea, todo), and makes it searchable.

"People take 50 notes a day but can only find 10 of them," said the founder. "QuickNote makes every note findable in under a second."

### Key Features

1. **Real-Time Transcription**: Whisper-quality transcription in 15+ languages
2. **Auto-Categorization**: AI assigns categories and tags without manual input
3. **Instant Search**: Full-text search across all notes, returning results in under 100ms

Pricing: Free for up to 100 notes/month. $9.99/month for unlimited.

Available on iOS and Android today. Web app coming Q3.
