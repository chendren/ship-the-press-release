# ParkSpot: Know Before You Go

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

ParkSpot launched a mobile app that shows real-time parking availability for any destination before you leave home.

"Americans spend 17 hours per year looking for parking," said the founder. "ParkSpot tells you exactly where to park and how much it'll cost before you start the car."

### 1. Real-Time Availability

Aggregates data from 50,000+ parking facilities, street sensors, and crowdsourced reports. Color-coded map: green (available), yellow (filling), red (full).

### 2. Price Comparison

Shows pricing for all options within walking distance: garages, lots, meters, and valet. Sorted by total cost including time walking.

### 3. Reserve and Pay

Book a guaranteed spot in supported garages. Pay through the app. No ticket, no meter — your license plate is your parking pass.

### Pricing

Free to search. $0.50 booking fee per reservation.

iOS and Android. Available in 25 US cities at launch.
