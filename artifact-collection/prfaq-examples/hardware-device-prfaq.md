# DeskSense: The Meeting Room Display That Actually Works

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**

DeskSense launched a 7-inch e-ink display for meeting room doors that syncs with Google Calendar and Microsoft 365, runs for 18 months on two AA batteries, and installs with 3M adhesive strips.

"Every meeting room display on the market requires PoE, a dedicated iPad, or a $500 device that needs firmware updates," said the founder. "DeskSense is a $79 display that you stick to a door and forget about."

### 1. E-Ink, No Charging

E-ink refreshes only when the schedule changes. 18-month battery life on 2 AA batteries. No power cable, no PoE, no charging dock.

### 2. Zero IT Overhead

WiFi setup via phone app (60 seconds). Calendar sync via OAuth — no admin consent needed for Google Workspace. Microsoft 365 requires admin consent once.

### 3. Walk-Up Booking

Tap the display to book the room for 15, 30, or 60 minutes. Capacitive touch through the e-ink panel. No app needed for walk-up bookings.

### Pricing

$79 per display. No subscription. Bulk pricing (10+): $69 each.
